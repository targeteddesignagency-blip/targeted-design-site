Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here's how we handle your information.

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Applies To: targeted-design.com and all related services

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information when you sign up for our services, place an order, or contact us:

  • Name — Your full name or the name of your authorized representative.
  • Email Address — For account communication, order confirmations, and service updates.
  • Phone Number — For account support and campaign coordination.
  • Business Name — The name of the business being advertised through our EDDM services.

We also collect standard technical information automatically when you visit our website, including IP address, browser type, device type, and pages visited. This information is used solely for analytics and site improvement.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our EDDM marketing services;
  • Process your orders and manage your account;
  • Design and produce your postcard marketing materials;
  • Communicate with you about your campaigns, billing, and account status;
  • Comply with legal obligations; and
  • Improve our website and services.

We do not use your personal information for purposes materially different from those disclosed in this Policy without providing you notice and, where required by law, obtaining your consent.

3. Payment Information

Payment for our services is processed through Square, Inc. We do not collect, store, or have access to your credit card number, bank account details, or other sensitive payment information. All payment data is handled directly by Square, which is PCI DSS compliant. Please review Square's privacy policy at squareup.com/legal/privacy for details on how your payment information is processed and protected.

4. Information Sharing

We share your personal information only with the third-party service providers necessary to deliver our EDDM marketing services:

  • Square, Inc. — Payment processing. Square receives your name, email, and payment details when you complete a transaction.
  • United States Postal Service (USPS) — Mailing services. USPS receives your business name and mailing information required for EDDM delivery.
  • Twilio Inc. — Communication services. Twilio may be used to send SMS notifications related to your account and campaigns.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing or advertising purposes. We may also disclose your information when required by law, to protect our rights, or to comply with a legal obligation.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active, plus an additional thirty (30) days after account closure or cancellation. After this retention period, your personal information is securely deleted or anonymized, except where retention is required by law (such as for tax and accounting records, which are retained for the period required by applicable law).

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access — You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction — You may request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
  • Deletion — You may request that we delete your personal information, subject to any legal retention requirements.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@targeted-design.com. We will respond to all verified requests within thirty (30) days.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and collect analytical data. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain features of the website.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services that are not owned or controlled by Targeted Design Agency. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website at targeted-design.com/privacy.html and, where practical, by sending an email to the address associated with your account at least thirty (30) days before the changes take effect.
