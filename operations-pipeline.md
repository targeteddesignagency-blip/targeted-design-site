### NOVA'S PARALLEL WORKSTREAMS

Once Sage identifies diamond leads (score 4-5), Nova launches 6 parallel efforts immediately:

#### Stream 1: Pre-Approach Research (Immediate)
For every score-5 lead:
- Owner name and background (Yelp reviews, Google reviews, LinkedIn)
- Business pain points from reviews ("hard to reach", "no answer", "voicemail full")
- Online presence audit (website? Google listing claimed? Social media?)
- Revenue estimate (industry average × employee count from reviews)
- Competitor map (who else serves this ZIP in same category?)

#### Stream 2: Industry Research (Parallel to Stream 1)
For each of our 8 industries:
- Average revenue and profit margins
- Top 3 pain points specific to that industry in bilingual markets
- Common AI automation use cases (appointments, follow-up, dispatch)
- ROI calculator: "$35K/yr receptionist vs $550/mo AI = save $28,400/yr"
- Competitor analysis: who else offers marketing/automation in SA?

#### Stream 3: Multi-Channel Outreach (Day 3-5)
For diamond leads (score 4-5):
- **Email**: Personalized cold email referencing their business name, ZIP, pain point
- **SMS**: Brief text with local hook ("Hey [name], saw [business] on [street]. Quick question...")
- **Social DM**: Where available (Facebook business pages, Instagram)
- **Voice warm-up**: Voice Agent calls during business hours, qualifies, books consultation
- All channels run IN PARALLEL, not sequential

#### Stream 4: Voice Agent Preparation (Parallel to Stream 3)
- Industry-specific greeting scripts (8 industries × 2 languages = 16 scripts)
- Qualification questions per industry
- Objection handling flows
- Booking confirmation templates
- Follow-up SMS templates (bilingual)

#### Stream 5: Print & Drop Logistics (Day 5-7)
- Card design → print vendor → USPS EDDM
- Route selection per ZIP (USPS carrier routes)
- Mailing statement generation
- Drop scheduling (Tuesday-Wednesday delivery)
- UTM tracking and call attribution setup

#### Stream 6: Attribution & Tracking (Parallel to all above)
- UTM parameters on all digital touchpoints
- Call tracking: (210) 903-5551 logged to Google Sheet
- Attribution: which ZIP, which card, which channel led to each call
- A/B testing framework for future card variations
- Dashboard: calls per ZIP, conversion rate, revenue per drop

### DECISION POINTS FOR NOVA

| Trigger | Decision | Rule |
|---------|----------|------|
| Lead score 5 | Launch all 6 streams immediately | Diamond = full court press |
| Lead score 4 | Launch streams 1, 3, 4 | Strong but not diamond |
| Lead score 3 | Add to email nurture only | Not ready for outreach |
| Lead score 1-2 | Park in sheet, revisit in Batch 002 | Wrong fit for this card |
| Owner name found | Personalize all outreach | Named outreach > generic |
| Spanish reviews found | Lead with Spanish outreach | Match the customer's language |
| No online presence | Higher priority — they NEED us | No website = no leads coming in |
| Voicemail full | Highest priority — they're LOSING customers | Crossover confirmed |
| Post-drop call volume low | Increase outreach frequency | Card isn't pulling, we push harder |
