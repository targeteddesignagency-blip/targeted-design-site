### EDDM Retail vs BMEU

| Feature | EDDM Retail | EDDM BMEU |
|---------|------------|-----------|
| Permit required | No | Yes ($265/yr) |
| Daily limit | 5,000 per ZIP | No limit |
| Postage rate | $0.247/piece | $0.242/piece |
| Drop-off | Local retail counter | Business Mail Entry Unit |
| Payment | Cash, check, card, online | Permit imprint, commercial account |

**Recommendation:** Start with EDDM Retail (4 diamond ZIPs × ~5,000 homes = ~20,000 pieces max per day, fitting the 5,000-per-ZIP limit). Switch to BMEU when dropping over 20,000 pieces.

### Top Rejection Reasons (AVOID)

1. **Wrong paper thickness** — 80lb gloss is minimum but cheap; use 14pt C2S
2. **Missing indicia text** — must have ECRWSS, EDDM, U.S. POSTAGE PAID, city/state
3. **Indicia wrong placement** — must be upper-right within 1.625"×1.375" zone
4. **Content in address zone** — lower-right 4.5"×2.75" must be clear for postal processing
5. **Handwritten/sticker indicia** — must be printed directly on the piece
6. **Standard postcard size** — 4×6 or 6×9 DO NOT QUALIFY for EDDM
