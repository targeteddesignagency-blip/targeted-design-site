## CARD LAYOUT NOTES

### General
- **Card Size:** 12" × 9" (USPS EDDM flat rate)
- **Colors:** #000000 (black bg), #fe1616 (red accent), #ffffff (white text), #333132 (dark grey boxes)
- **Logo:** Centered, 140px height, above headline
- **Font:** Bold sans-serif (Montserrat or similar), large and readable at arm's length
- **Headline:** 36-48pt, ALL CAPS, two-line hook
- **Industry boxes:** 4×2 grid, each with icon + industry name, dark grey bg (#333132), white text
- **CTA:** Phone number in red (#fe1616), 24pt+, below the grid
- **Price anchor:** "Starting at $150" in white, smaller text
- **Guarantee line:** "Results Guaranteed" / "Resultados Garantizados" — reduces perceived risk

### Front (English)
- Hook: "YOUR PHONE SHOULD BE RINGING. NOT YOUR COMPETITOR'S."
- Subtext: "EVERY DOOR. EVERY HOME. YOUR NEXT CUSTOMER."
- This speaks to the business owner's pain: empty phone, competitor stealing their customers

### Back (Spanish)
- Hook: "SU TELÉFONO DEBERÍA ESTAR SONANDO. NO EL DE SU COMPETENCIA."
- Subtext: "CADA PUERTA. CADA HOGAR. SU PRÓXIMO CLIENTE."
- Direct translation that hits identically in Spanish

### Voice Agent Follow-Up
- When they call (210) 903-5551, Voice Agent answers:
  - English: "Targeted Design Agency, this is Alex. How can I help you grow your business today?"
  - Spanish: "Agencia Targeted Design, soy Alex. ¿Cómo puedo ayudarle a hacer crecer su negocio hoy?"
- Qualifies lead → books consultation → upsells AI automation ($550/mo Standard / $1,000/mo Premium)

### Why This Works
1. **8 industries** = broad appeal across our diamond ZIPs
2. **Bilingual** = 1 card reaches both languages, halves our print cost
3. **Phone number CTA** = Voice Agent answers 24/7, no lead falls through
4. **$150 anchor** = impulse buy for businesses that have never done marketing
5. **"Results Guaranteed"** = eliminates risk objection before they call
6. **4 diamond ZIPs** = 17,000+ households, 2,000+ businesses, 85-95% Hispanic
