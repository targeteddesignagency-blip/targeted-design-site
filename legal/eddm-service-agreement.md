## 📋 The Bootstrap Model

**No annual contracts. No lock-in. No early termination fees.**

Month-to-month means:
- Either party can cancel with 30 days notice
- No penalty for leaving
- We can adjust pricing with 30 days notice
- Lower friction to close (clients aren't committing to a year)
- Same legal protections (IP, liability, indemnification)

**Why This Works:**
- Clients who are happy will stay (no need to lock them in)
- Clients who leave weren't a good fit anyway
- Cash flow is the same
- Flexibility to pivot pricing/packages
- Trust-based relationship (partnership, not prison)

## 1. SERVICES

### 1.1 Scope of Services

Service Provider agrees to provide Every Door Direct Mail (EDDM) marketing services as selected below:

**☐ ECONOMY TIER — $150/month**
- Quarter-page ad design (provided by Client or template-based)
- Monthly mail drop to 5,000 households
- USPS EDDM routing and delivery coordination
- Basic performance tracking (delivery confirmation)

**☐ BASELINE TIER — $550/month** ⭐ MOST POPULAR
- Half-page custom ad design
- Monthly mail drop to 5,000 households
- USPS EDDM routing and delivery coordination
- QR code tracking integration
- Monthly performance report (scan rates, estimated reach)
- A/B testing of ad creative (quarterly)

**☐ PREMIUM TIER — $1,000/month**
- Full postcard custom design (both sides)
- Monthly mail drop to 5,000 households (up to 10,000 upon request)
- USPS EDDM routing and delivery coordination
- QR code tracking integration
- Weekly performance report with analytics dashboard access
- A/B testing of ad creative (monthly)
- Dedicated account manager
- Priority design turnaround (48 hours)

### 1.2 Service Specifications

- **Mail Drop Frequency:** Monthly (once per calendar month)
- **Household Count:** 5,000 households per drop (Premium: up to 10,000)
- **Geographic Targeting:** Client-selected ZIP codes and carrier routes
- **Design Approval:** Client must approve ad creative before printing
- **Printing:** Included in service fee (standard cardstock, 4-color)
- **Postage:** Included in service fee (USPS EDDM rates)
- **Delivery:** Via USPS EDDM program (100% delivery guarantee to addressed routes)

### 1.3 Service Commencement

Services shall commence within **14 business days** of:
(a) Execution of this Agreement, AND
(b) Receipt of first month's payment, AND
(c) Client provision of all required materials (logos, brand guidelines, content)

## 2. TERM AND TERMINATION

### 2.1 Month-to-Month Term

This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue on a **month-to-month basis**, unless earlier terminated as provided herein.

### 2.2 Termination for Convenience

**Either Party** may terminate this Agreement at any time, for any reason, upon **thirty (30) days** written notice to the other Party.

**Example:** Notice given May 15 → Termination effective June 14 → Final mail drop May 30 (if already in production).

### 2.3 Termination for Cause

Either Party may terminate this Agreement **immediately** upon written notice if the other Party:
(a) Materially breaches any provision and fails to cure within **fifteen (15) days** of written notice;
(b) Becomes insolvent, files for bankruptcy, or ceases business operations;
(c) Engages in fraudulent or illegal activities related to this Agreement.

### 2.4 Effect of Termination

Upon termination:
(a) Service Provider shall complete any mail drops already in production (if feasible);
(b) Client shall pay all outstanding fees through the termination date;
(c) Service Provider shall deliver to Client all final performance reports;
(d) Each Party shall return or destroy confidential information of the other Party;
(e) **No early termination fee** — month-to-month means either party can leave with 30 days notice.

### 2.5 Pricing Changes

Service Provider may adjust monthly fees upon **thirty (30) days** written notice. If Client does not accept new pricing, Client may terminate effective when price change takes effect (no penalty).
